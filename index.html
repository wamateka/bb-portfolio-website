Activities

Let's get away from the four-walled classroom setting to better understand what motivates me to get out of bed with a little bit of insight on who I am through some of the activities I enjoy and which have shaped part of who I am today.

During the summer, I usually volunteer at Nacham Africa, a non-governmental organization that cares for the uncared for in public hospitals, through various programs such as providing them with food, soap, and other life necessities, entertaining children in designated areas to allow hospitalized parents to rest, interacting with the patients for morale, and assisting them in reintegrating the society by equipping them with skills such as sewing knowledge. Volunteering at Nacham Africa is an enriching experience as It brings to me a different perspective of life, one through the eyes of the most destitute. It has taught me to always be humble, grateful, and most importantly, compassionate.
